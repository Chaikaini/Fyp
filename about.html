About Us

The Seeds Learning Centre

Established in 2015, The Seeds Learning Centre is a premier tutoring center located in Kulai, Johor Darul Ta’zim. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds and helping students achieve academic excellence through personalized and engaging learning experiences.

Fun Beyond Learning

At The Seeds Learning Centre, we believe in holistic development. Apart from academic tutoring, we organize fun and educational activities for our students (subject to availability and conditions).

Visiting an Orchard

We take students to a local orchard to explore nature, learn about fruits, and understand the importance of agriculture (available during harvest season).

Celebrating Christmas

We organize fun playtime and shared meals with students, fostering friendship and joy in a relaxed environment.

Playing at the Park

We take students to a local park for fun outdoor play, encouraging physical activity and social bonding (organized during New Year festivities).
